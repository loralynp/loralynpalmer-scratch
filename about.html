ABOUT ME

Loralyn Palmer is a finance student at Brigham Young University, planning to graduate in 2027. She is passionate about many areas of finance and aspires to specialize in wealth management.

At BYU, she is actively involved in several organizations, including the BYU Finance Society, the BYU Women in Finance Association, and the Private Banking & Wealth Management Club. She is also dedicated to serving others and participates in Best Buddies, a program focused on building genuine friendships and fostering community inclusion.

Loralyn enjoys working in team environments and has successfully led multiple group projects. She values collaboration, seeks diverse perspectives, and is committed to continuous learning and growth. Her passion for making a positive impact is evident in both her academic pursuits and her community involvement.

Outside of academics, Loralyn enjoys spending time with friends, practicing pilates, golfing, eating good food, and solving puzzles. She is always excited to find new hobbies and build meaningful connections with those around her. She cares about making a positive impact and deeply cares about the people in her life.
